The NULLx Culture

Our core philosophy is Change for the better. More specifically, we have great people working together as a dream team. With this approach, we are a more flexible, fun, stimulating, creative, collaborative and successful organization.

Open and constructive

We strive to communicate clearly and share helpful feedback.

Ideas over hierarchy

We evaluate any ideas to shape and improve them through candid debate, and to take them from concept to action.

Celebrate differences

We encourage different opinions and approaches to be heard, and then we come together and build.

Learn and Be Curious

We never stop learning and always seek to improve ourselves. We are curious about new possibilities and act to explore them.

Insist on the Highest Standards

We always want to follow standards for developing or solving problems which ensure the highest quality.

Orientation to Time

We set short solid goals, develop plans, and work hard and persevere to achieve our ambitions.
